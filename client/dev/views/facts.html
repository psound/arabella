DRIVING TIPS

Becoming a safer, less distracted driver takes a combination of skill and practice. Here are a few tips to use every time you get behind the wheel.
Put your phone away.
Avoid temptation by silencing your phone and putting it out of reach.
Hands on the wheel.
Anytime you take your hands off the wheel, there’s a good chance something’s distracting you.
If you’re driving, be the driver.
As hard as it is to resist your fellow passengers, it’s your job to keep everyone safe. That means keeping your attention on the road.
Tune in.
It’s easy to let music take your mind off the road. Just keep it low and don’t get too carried away with the beat.
