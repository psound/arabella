THE TOUR

Distractology’s traveling tour puts inexperienced drivers behind the wheel of a simulator to experience real-world distractions that can cause real-world accidents. Like spilling a drink in your lap. Fiddling with the radio. And, of course, texting. You know, distractions.

Help us put an end to accidents by getting schooled in Distractology. Take the tour, complete the Challenge, and be in charge when you’re behind the wheel.
